About us

Ortho Digital Lab is a full-service orthodontic laboratory skilled in the fabrication of orthodontic appliances, retainers and splints using the most advanced technologies. When you have a complex case, our staff is available to assist with case consultation and custom appliance fabrication.

Our quality craftsmanship and commitment to service have made Ortho Digital Lab the growing choice for orthodontists and dentists nationwide.

Our Laboratory produces the Best Quality Dental appliances and retainers, harnessing the power of the most accurate and reliable dental 3D Printing.

Minimizing delivery times and costs, optimizing the internal work flow that allows us to provide Comfort, Speed, a valuable Service and satisfaction to our clients and patients.

We will make your desired retainer, expander or aligner with just a simple 3D scan.
We offer free printing with the production of any appliance.
